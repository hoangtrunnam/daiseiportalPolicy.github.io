Dasei portal Peer Bonus System Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
Our Daisei portal peer bonus system is an in-house, dedicated application for performance evaluation and feedback among employees.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information
The Daisei Portal Peer Bonus System is an application for internal use only and does not collect or store any personal information outside of the user. However, information about your activity within the app, such as your application usage history and feedback, may be collected

3. Cookies and Tracking
Applications may use cookies and tracking technologies to understand usage and improve the user experience. The information collected is only used to improve the functionality of the application and to diagnose problems.

4. Protection of Information
We have implemented appropriate security measures to ensure that the information in the application is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. This includes the implementation of encryption technologies, access control, and regular security audits.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If there is a change, we will notify the relevant departments and personnel. In addition, if there is an important change, we may notify the entire company.

6. Inquiries
If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy policy of the Daisei Portal Peer Bonus System, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
